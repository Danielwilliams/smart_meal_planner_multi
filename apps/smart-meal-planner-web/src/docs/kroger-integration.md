# Kroger Integration Guide

## Overview

Smart Meal Planner integrates with Kroger to make shopping easier. This guide explains how to connect your Kroger account and use the integration features.

## Connecting Your Kroger Account

### Step 1: Initiate Connection
- Navigate to the Shopping List or Cart page
- Click the "Connect to Kroger" button
- You'll be redirected to Kroger's official login page

### Step 2: Authenticate with Kroger
- Log in with your Kroger credentials on the official Kroger website
- Authorize Smart Meal Planner to access your account when prompted
- You will be redirected back to Smart Meal Planner after successful authorization

### Step 3: Select Your Store
- After connecting, you'll be prompted to select your preferred Kroger store
- This store will be used when searching for products and adding items to your cart

## Using Kroger Integration

### Adding Items to Your Kroger Cart
1. View your shopping list
2. Click "Add to Kroger Cart"
3. Items will be matched with Kroger products and added to your cart
4. Review the matched items before completing the process
5. Continue shopping on Kroger's website or app to complete your purchase

### Searching Kroger Products
- When viewing recipes or shopping lists, you can see Kroger product matches
- Prices and availability are specific to your selected store
- Product information is updated in real-time from Kroger

## Security Information

### OAuth Authentication
Smart Meal Planner uses OAuth 2.0 to connect to your Kroger account:
- You authenticate directly with Kroger's website
- Smart Meal Planner never sees or stores your Kroger password
- Only access tokens with limited permissions are stored

### Data Usage
- We only access data necessary for shopping list functionality
- Your Kroger account information is never shared with third parties
- You can disconnect your Kroger account at any time

## Troubleshooting

### Connection Issues
- **"Unable to connect to Kroger"**: Try again in a few minutes or check your internet connection
- **"Authentication failed"**: Ensure you're using the correct Kroger credentials
- **"Session expired"**: Your connection may have timed out; reconnect your account

### Cart Issues
- **"Items not found"**: Some items may not be available at your selected store
- **"Unable to add to cart"**: Check that your Kroger connection is still active
- **"Price discrepancies"**: Prices may change between viewing and checkout

## FAQ

### Do I need to reconnect my account every time?
No, once connected, your Kroger account stays linked until you disconnect it or the authorization expires (typically after 30 days).

### Is my Kroger password stored in Smart Meal Planner?
No, we never store your Kroger password. Authentication happens directly on Kroger's website.

### How do I disconnect my Kroger account?
Go to Profile Settings > Connected Accounts > Disconnect Kroger Account.

### Can I use multiple Kroger stores?
Yes, you can change your preferred store at any time from the Settings page.

### What if I don't have a Kroger account?
You can still use Smart Meal Planner without connecting to Kroger. You'll just need to shop manually.
